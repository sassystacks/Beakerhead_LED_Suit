Pin connection for SD card board:

Teensy		SD Card
pin 10 -------> CS
pin 11 -------> DI
pin 12 -------> DO
pin 13 -------> CLK
5v -----------> 5V
Grnd ---------> Grnd

RF connection:
pin 2 --------> data

pin Connection for Loop Lights:

pin 4 --------> small circle
pin 5 --------> large circle
pin 6 --------> led strips
